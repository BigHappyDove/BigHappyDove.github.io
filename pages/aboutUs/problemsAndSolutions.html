Our problems and the solutions we came up with

In this section we will talk about the difficulties we have faced, and how we supported each other through the though times in order to find a solution to our problems.

Human problems

During this period, the group has had to face some difficulties linked to morale. However, our team spirit enabled us to get through hard times, and we, as a group, and for some, even as individuals, are stronger for it. The way we supported each other is a good lesson for our futures. Indeed, once we become engineers, we will probably have to manage a team, and good communication skills, as well as team spirit, will be great assets.

Coding problems

We have tackled more complex functions these past few months. Thus, we have faced more difficulties, and sometimes, one member of the group alone could not find a solution. There again, the support of our team mates was key for us to unlock difficult situations.
